This is the "website" of Robert Wilcox, a Ph.D. student in Mathematics at the University of South Carolina. It's really just a place for me to put random projects on the internet. Check out the Derivative Calculator I am working on. It should find the derivative with interactive explanation of any function you might possibly run across in a Calculus class. I am also adding functionality so that it will guess where you made an error in your differentiation, but I have only added in what I consider the most common errors and it will only work if you make at most one error for now. Have some fun and see if you can break either of them! Email me at rwilcox5@gmail.com if you have any suggestions for improvements.
