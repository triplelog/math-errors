# Logarithms
log(x) or ln(x) are both ln(x)
log_b(x) or logb(x) have base b.
loglog(x) or lnln(x) is ln(ln(x)).
logloglog(x) is ln(ln(ln(x))).
lnln(x) is ln(ln(x)).
loglog_b(x) is log_b(log_b(x)).
logloglog_b(x) is log_b(log_b(log_b(x))).
log^3(x) is (ln(x))^3
